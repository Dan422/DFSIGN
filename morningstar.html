The Sun is but a Morning Star

Morningstar was my first big design job during college. The company's name came from Henry David Thoreau's last line of 'Walden; Or, Life in the Woods' — "The sun is but a morning star". Beautiful, right?

A lot of what Thoreau wrote I found in their design language: natural, bright, and breathable. In my two years there, I learned from creating brand systems, experimenting with grids and layout, and working with great mentors. Oh, and leftover bagels aren't really that bad.
