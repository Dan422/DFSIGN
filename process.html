Process

1 Empathize

Who are we solving for and what are their problems?

Get to know who the users are and understand their problems through 1-on-1's, empathy maps, personas, surveys, and gathering feedback for patterns.

2 Ideate

How do we solve for them?

Come up with ways to solve their problems and improve experiences with storyboards, scenarios, team idea gathering, and design sprints.

3 Design

What will it look like and do?

Design, iterate, and build solutions through sketches, wireframes, prototypes, user testing, and development.

4 Improve

What can be improved?

Revisit, empathize, and continuously improve through metrics gathering, incremental and iterative deployment, and feedback loops.
