Process

1 Empathize

Who are we solving for? What are their problems?

Observe, discuss, and understand users/clients with empathy maps, persona charts, surveying, and gathering feedback for patterns.

2 Ideate

How do we solve for them?

Come up with ways to solve problems and improve experiences with user story maps, design sprints, team converging, and whiteboarding sessions.

3 Design

What will it look like and do?

Design, iterate, and build solutions through sketches, wireframes, prototypes, user testing, and development.

4 Improve

What can be improved?

Revisit, empathize, and continuously improve through metrics gathering, incremental and iterative deployment, and feedback loops.
