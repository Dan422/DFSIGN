About Dan



As a multi-disciplinary designer based in Phoenix, Dan loves balancing user and business needs to solve complex problems, design delightful products, and generate excelling outcomes.

He thrives by working closely with people: users, stakeholders, developers, researchers, and anyone involved during the entire product lifecycle. By being conscientious and a servant leader, Dan obsesses over design, advocates for users, and goes above and beyond to deliver fantastic experiences.

After 10 years of designing for finance, technology, fitness, and e-commerce, Dan is now a UX Designer at Endurance International Group. He learns and solves for small business owners by designing a web platform that helps grow their online presence and business.

Outside of work, Dan loves staying active by hitting the gym, running, golfing, and hiking. As a geek, he enjoys building computers, playing N64, and learning new coding languages. He also watches his Chicago Bears, White Sox, and Blackhawks play whenever they're on.

His wife, daughter, and dog are everything and inspire him to be the best husband, father, and ball-thrower he can be. Taking family road trips, renting a cabin for the weekend with friends, and visiting extended family across the pond has also sparked his interest in travel and learning abroad.
