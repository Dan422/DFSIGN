Dan Fournier

I'm a professional graphic and UX designer with a passion in crafting logical and thoughtful design solutions and experiences.


As a kid I built Legos and sketched cartoons. Creativity was second nature to me and I wanted to wield it. My interest in graphic design and typography was sparked when I took a graphics class in high school — I immediately fell in love with everything about it.

At the University of Illinois at Chicago I was fortunate enough to be influenced by world-renowned professionals in one of the most creative cities in the world, and my passion and understanding for design grew immensely.

After working in a leadership role for more than eight years within various industries, I've taken on many projects that've taught me how much design truly influences our world.

Skills

Obsessed with Design: Sharp and thorough understanding of design, including layout, typography, grid, and system development.

User-First: Proficient in human-centric UX practices and design thinking, from researching data, story-boarding, and gathering feedback loops, to crafting information architecture, user journeys, and rapid prototyping.

Always Inspired: In-depth experience with web, iOS, and Material design applications, guidelines, and trends.

Always Creating: Strong expertise in Photoshop, XD, Illustrator, Sketch, Invision, After Effects, and InDesign.

Always Building: Advanced in HTML and CSS/SASS web development with an understanding of Javascript and JQuery application.
