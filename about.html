About Dan


I was obsessed with building my own Legos and drawing cartoons as a kid. Creativity was second nature to me and I knew I had to wield it. My interest in design was sparked when I took a graphics class in high school — everything about it just clicked for me.

At the University of Illinois at Chicago I was fortunate enough to work alongside world-renowned professionals in one of the most creative cities in the world, and my passion and understanding for design and UX grew immensely.

After a decade of working in the finance, marketing, and technology industries, I've found my home in Scottsdale, Arizona working as a UX Designer for Endurance Group. My job is to design web applications that help small businesses with their online presence.
