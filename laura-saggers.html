Laura Saggers

Brand / UI / Print / Front End

Design work for Laura's growing music brand and various projects, events, and collaborations.

Design "Fun"

Laura is a dear friend of mine and an incredible talent. Her bubbly personality and optimism inspired me to design "fun."

With a bright combo of pinks, yellows, and blues, and referencing her LA-based location and British background as themes, I wanted to build an identity that, like her performances, brought joy, hilarity, and energy to her audiences.
