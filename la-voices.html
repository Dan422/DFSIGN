LA Voices

Brand / UI and UX / Print / Front End

Complete identity, web, and print design overhaul for an emerging LA-based voice camp and business.

Think of the Kids!

Working with this 80s-themed voice camp was a blast. Not only were they fun and energetic, but offered great insight into what their design needs were and how I should craft the language of the brand.

After some initial work, themes started to emerge: hip, vibrant, "poppy" as they put it. By coming up with a flat black and white primary base, the bright neon purple, teal, and orange cut through and complimented their activities well.

Visit Website

LA Voices is Santa Monica's first a cappella show choir, where talented young singers of all ages are exposed to the very best vocal training, encouraged in their passion for singing, and empowered to find their voice.
