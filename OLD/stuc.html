STUC Typeface

Typeface / Print

Senior year typeface project with an elementary school theme.

STUC is a physical typeface meant to represent two ideologies: the elementary and naive values comprised within a grade school education, and the static nature of uniform yet conflicting objects.

STUC is constructed with #2 standard pencils and pink bubble gum, materials that not only reflect the youthful atmoshpere of a grade school classroom, but also the uniformity embedded within the objects themselves.
