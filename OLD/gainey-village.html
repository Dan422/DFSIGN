The SHOPS Gainey Village

Brand / Print

Branding and print work for a luxury shopping center's holiday identity.

Winter in the Desert

I love the holidays. Cheer in the air, eggnog in the glass, and...70 degrees with sunshine?

The SHOPS is a small, luxury-focused shopping plaza nestled in central Scottsdale. For their holiday campaign, I wanted to focus on what winter in Arizona was like: crisp, warming, and refreshing.

By using a modern, clean sans-serif font with distinct color palletes of reds, greys, black, and yellows, I wanted to portray holiday cheer but with a firm nod to the desert landscape.

Located in the heart of Scottsdale, the SHOPS Gainey Village offers a unique blend of distinctive boutique shopping, trendy eateries and fine dining experiences, as well as specialty services.
