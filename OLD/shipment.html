Shipment

Brand / UI and UX

Branding, design, and usability studies for a parcel tracking app prototype.

Multiple Destinations

Shipment was my first adventure into mobile app design. Dreamt up by a developer coleague of mine, we wanted to prototype a product that eases the hassle of parcel tracking by providing timely destination alerts and automation.

After multiple cycles of usability studies, wireframing, and mockups, we came up with a great candidate to share with the community.

From a branding perspective, I wanted to portay the complexity of package delivery and not all routes are a straight line from A to B. As consumers, we take for granted how difficult it is to ship many packages across long distances, from clicking purchase to door.
