The Collector's House

Brand / UI / Print / Front End

Identity, web, and print design for a growing luxury brand and boutique in Scottsdale, Arizona.

Rough Luxury

In 2015, my client had a vision to open a lifestyle, luxury, and interior design gallery that celebrated "artists at work."

From branding to full page spreads for Phoenix Home & Garden, I was compelled by the imagery we used of every day folks like us getting their hands dirty to build beautiful, one-of-a-kind pieces.

Rough hands, natural and organic material, and traditional roots. These were what inspired me to craft The Collector's House design language and collateral.

The Collector's House was developed with a focus on quality of life through high-end and original interiors. We are an intimate, well curated lifestyle & interiors gallery.
