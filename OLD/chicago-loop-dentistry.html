Chicago Loop Dentistry

Brand / Print

Identity and print work for an emerging dentistry practice in Chicago, IL.

Do the Floss

Instead of trying to pull elements from the West Loop area of Chicago, I wanted to go more literal, weaving dental floss in a looping pattern to form the main mark of the logo.

The color was inspired by "the walls of your doctor's office" palette but with a brighter twist.
