Chalk It Up

Brand

Identity refresh for a local custom furniture and home decor startup.

Back to the Chalkboard

My client has a passion for refurbishing antique furniture into beautiful custom pieces for homes. They wanted to convey not only this passion, but also the process and what goes into each piece.

I approached this branding project knowing that process is everything to a lot of people, even when consumers don't see it from the surface. By drawing elements like paint, wood, and hammer, combined with a hearty, curved typeface, Chalk It Up became an entity just like their pieces: one-of-a-kind.
